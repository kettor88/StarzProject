Services We Offer

We offer Clinical Activities, Eye Clinic program, Nutrition Program, Community Health Activities, Co-Investment Program

Clinical Activities

RPI aims to create a replicable health care model for rural poor and urban slums communities in Liberia, with the goal of reducing the high maternal neonatal and child mortality rate and making quality rate and making quality health care accessible to all. Our vision is a model of affordable, accessible, and quality care for the poor.

Eye Clinic program

In 2021 RPI’s Eye care program, with the support of it partners GoodVision USA and Foundation L’Occitane, was able to relaunch on strong basis. The eye program have screen over 5259 number of patients in Montserrado and dispense 506 number of glasses to adult and 251 glasses students.

Nutrition Program

RPI provided Nutrition education programs aim to stay, avoid or reduce the prevalence of these chronic and life threatening conditions. Nutrition education can take many forms from one-on-one sessions to large group classes. Taught by experienced and educated individuals.

Saving Mother and children lives

Community health workers’ routine tracking of pregnant women for ANC visit has resulted to Zero maternal death and over 2000+ deliveries were carry out successful without any Complex problems.

Immunization Services

RPI is offering free immunization to children and pregnant women within our catchment area and from around Monrovia. In 2021 were able to immunize 1,322 under 5 children and 749 pregnant women.

COVID-19 Community Engagement Activities

As part of our response to the third wave of COVID-19 in Liberia, RIP carried out a community awareness campaign. Sensitizing community dwellers on the need for washing hands every day, wearing masks, and practicing social distancing. This campaign was launched in the presence of community leaders, representatives from the country's IMS on risk communication.

About Us

Refuge Place International (RPI) is a registered non-governmental organization both in the USA and Liberia located in Bassa Town, Lower Johnsonville
which has provided free and low-cost health services within its catchment area since 2014.
RPI’s core values include empowerment of the poor, compassion, accountability, transparency, and partnership.
Our vision is a model of affordable, accessible and quality care for the poor
